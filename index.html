About

Discover more on Instagram
The Worm’s Memoirs by Zeena is a 2D point-and-click narrative game exploring themes of trauma, fear, and recovery. The game follows “the Worm,” the protagonist, as they go about their mundane daily tasks, such as tending to a deteriorating house. However, the story unfolds as the Worm navigates two parallel worlds—one filled with fear and the other offering euphoric escape. The game is heavily influenced by psychological horror, with the main challenge being whether the Worm will confront their fears or seek safety in illusions​.

This project, which is still in development, touches on emotional and psychological themes, including Metathesiophobia (the fear of moving on). Players are given the choice of exploring the dark or light sides of the Worm’s world, influencing the narrative’s direction.
